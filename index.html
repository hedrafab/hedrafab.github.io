Our Story

Sri Murugan Transport is a petroleum products' transportation company currently holding multiple contracts in government and private companies. Mr Murugan's hands-on experience in the field at the beginning of his career combined with his natural management skills has been the driving force behind SMT Group's success. Over the years, with pure hard work, determination and in-depth knowledge in the field, SMT Group was able to build up a reputation as a reliable and trustworthy company. Despite the competition rich transportation field, SMT Group has been able to flourish amongst its competitors due to the highly experienced management staff who operate under Mr Murugan's guidance. SMT Group comprises of several transportation companies, including but not limited to, Hedra Logistics, Sri Karthik Transport and Sri Senthil Kumar Transport.

Our Services

SMT Group is dedicated to provide safe and timely transport of goods. We understand that on-time delivery and safety of goods is your number one priority. We make it our mission to fulfil your requirements as quick and as safe as possible to provide you with maximum profits. Our diligent management team can foresee issues that may arise before or during the transport of your goods due to their vast knowledge and experience in the field.
